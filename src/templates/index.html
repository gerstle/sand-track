Welcome to The Sand Track

Sand City Racing Club

A friendly paragliding race circuit for (mostly) Monterey Bay.

Welcome

The Sand City Racing Club is an open group focused on fostering community, sharing knowledge, and having fun with your fellow paragliders. We welcome everyone to join us for some friendly racing. We hope to encourage you to push your flying boundaries, learn how to use your instruments, practice flying tasks, and have fun while flying our beloved Monterey Bay dunes.

How it works

  • There's no sign-up or fees, but you can always buy us a beer if you'd like.
  • Tasks are usually open for a week to a month.
  • Fly them whenever you want.
  • On whatever wing you want.
  • Upload as many tracklogs as you want, but you only get one entry per task per glider class. The best one is always chosen.
  • Scoring is done solely by time.
  • The clock starts when you exit the Start of Speed Section cylinder and ends when you enter the End of Speed Section cylinder.
  • Yes, you still have to make it to GOAL.

Getting Started

  1. Download the sand-city-v1.wpt waypoint file from the "Files" download menu and load it on your flight computer.
  2. Click on the Tasks link to see a list of tasks.
  3. Find the task you want to fly and click "Details" on that task.
  4. Either put in the turnpoints manually or use the QR code to load the task into your flight computer.
  5. Go fly the task!
  6. Upload your tracklog (.igc file) on the task details page.
  7. Checkout Doctor Doug's youtube video below for a more visual walk through.
